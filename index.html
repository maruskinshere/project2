$HAMC

$HAMC is a token created on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) with an unlimited total emission, functioning as a digital asset operating on the BSC blockchain. $HAMC serves as the key token within the house of Hamsters ecosystem, managing the in-game economy and ensuring its stability, enabling players to monetize their activities and play to earn.

Detailed Tokenomics

Transparency

House of Hamsters uses the cutting-edge power of blockchain technology to document all transactions in a transparent, public ledger. Thanks to the open source shuffling and dealing of cards, we provide a completely open and fair competition.

Game Performance

Earn $HAMC tokens while playing and watch your rewards grow with your gaming skills! The more adept you become at the game, the greater the amount of HAMC tokens you’ll earn.

Free Trade

Tokens can be easily exchanged on the DEX and CEX. This feature allows you to buy and sell tokens outside of our platform.
